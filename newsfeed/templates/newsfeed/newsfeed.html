PUP-Office of Guidance and Counseling

The PUP Office of Guidance is dedicated to fostering mental health and personal growth within the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. Committed to enhancing emotional well-being, the office provides accessible counseling, support services, and programs tailored to empower students and staff in navigating life's challenges.

Confidentiality

We value your privacy and we assure that every information you provide is treated as confidential.

Competence

We work with compassionate and skilled counselors committed to fostering a positive impact on your mental well-being.

Compassion

Our counselors are here to listen to your concerns without judgment, wholeheartedly dedicated to helping you navigate and alleviate your challenges.
